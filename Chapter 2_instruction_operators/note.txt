C Operator Precedence (Highest to Lowest)

1. Postfix
   ()  []  ->  .  expr++  expr--

2. Unary
   ++expr  --expr  +  -  !  ~  (type)  *  &  sizeof

3. Multiplicative
   *  /  %

4. Additive
   +  -

5. Shift
   <<  >>

6. Relational
   <  <=  >  >=

7. Equality
   ==  !=

8. Bitwise AND
   &

9. Bitwise XOR
   ^

10. Bitwise OR
    |

11. Logical AND
    &&

12. Logical OR
    ||

13. Conditional (Ternary)
    ?:

14. Assignment
    =  +=  -=  *=  /=  %=  <<=  >>=  &=  ^=  |=

15. Comma
    ,
